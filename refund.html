Refund Policy

CDEF – Credit | Debit | Equal | Fund. Clear guidelines on our fee structure, refund eligibility, and billing practices for all financial services.

CDEF – Credit | Debit | Equal | Fund ("CDEF", "Company", "we", "us", or "our") operates primarily on a "No Recovery, No Fee" basis, emphasizing our commitment to successful outcomes for our clients. This Refund Policy outlines the fees, charges, and conditions under which refunds may be considered.

Fee Structure

No Recovery, No Fee Policy

Our primary fee structure is contingent upon the successful recovery of funds. If no money is recovered on behalf of the client, no collection fee will be charged.

0% if unsuccessful

Minimum Fees

A minimum fee applies in cases where the fees earned would be less than the baseline amount, ensuring fair compensation for services rendered.

₹2,500 minimum

Skip Tracing Fees

Skip tracing and location services are charged per instance. These fees are payable in advance and are not covered under the "No Recovery, No Fee" policy.

₹5,000 per case

Recovery Fees

Fees for monies recovered are charged at an agreed rate between CDEF and the client. Standard rates apply in the absence of a specific agreement.

Up to 25% + taxes
Ancillary Charges: Charges for legal consultation, court fees, and skip tracing services are considered ancillary charges and are payable by the client as applicable.

Deductions and Payments

CDEF may deduct any fees or ancillary charges from monies held in connection with the payment of a debt, including debts recovered on behalf of associated companies of the client.

All fee deductions are transparently documented and communicated to the client prior to processing. Itemized statements are provided for all transactions.

Legal Action and Court Fees

CDEF may recommend experienced lawyers and legal professionals for legal actions. While clients may choose their own legal representation, CDEF's costs, fees, and ancillary charges remain payable as per the service agreement.

CDEF will act as an agent for liaising with legal professionals, managing communications, and facilitating the legal process. Court fees and additional charges for these services are payable by the client and may be recoverable as legal costs depending on the terms of business with the client's debtors.

Refund Eligibility

  • Skip Tracing Fees: Refunds for skip tracing fees are not available once the skip tracing service has commenced or been initiated.
  • Collection Fees: In line with our "No Recovery, No Fee" policy, collection fees are only applicable upon successful fund recovery. No refunds are necessary as no fees are charged in the absence of successful recovery.
  • Ancillary and Legal Charges: Fees for legal actions, court fees, and other ancillary charges are not refundable, as they are incurred for services rendered and expenses paid on behalf of the client.
  • Advance Payments: Any advance payments made for specific services that are subsequently not rendered may be eligible for partial or full refund, subject to review and approval.

Billing Errors and Disputes

In the event of a billing error or dispute regarding fees charged, clients are encouraged to contact CDEF immediately. We are committed to resolving all billing concerns fairly and promptly.

All disputes will be reviewed within 7-10 business days, and appropriate resolutions will be communicated to the client.

Termination of Agreement

In the event of agreement termination by either party, all fees and ancillary charges incurred up to the point of termination become immediately payable. Any outstanding recoveries in process will be concluded according to the terms of the original agreement.

Clients terminating the agreement after services have commenced may be liable for pro-rated fees based on work completed.

Contact and Dispute Resolution

For questions, concerns, or disputes related to fees, charges, or this Refund Policy, clients are encouraged to contact CDEF directly. We are committed to fair and transparent billing practices, ensuring our clients are fully informed throughout our partnership.

Contact CDEF

Have questions about our refund policy or billing practices? Our team is here to help you understand your financial obligations.

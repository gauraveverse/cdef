FAQ

Payment Recovery Questions

What is CDEF and how can it help my business?+
CDEF supports businesses in recovering outstanding payments from vendors, distributors, or clients after goods or services have been delivered. We step in when internal follow-ups fail and initiate a structured recovery process to secure your dues.
When should I escalate a case to CDEF?+
If payments are delayed beyond agreed terms, reminders are being ignored, or communication has stopped completely, it is time to escalate. Early intervention significantly increases recovery probability.
How does the recovery process work?+
After reviewing your invoices and supporting documents, our team verifies the claim and initiates formal communication with the defaulting party. The process may involve structured negotiation, repayment planning, written notices, or legal coordination where required.
Will CDEF damage my business relationships?+
Our approach is professional and compliance-driven. The objective is recovery through structured dialogue and documented communication. Wherever possible, disputes are resolved through negotiation without unnecessary escalation.
What documents are required to start recovery?+
You will typically need invoices, delivery proof, purchase orders or agreements, communication records, and payment terms. The stronger your documentation, the stronger your recovery position.
Does CDEF handle disputed payments?+
Yes. If a payment is under dispute, we assess the claim, review documentation, and facilitate structured negotiation between both parties to reach a resolution or settlement.
Is the recovery process legally compliant?+
All recovery activities are conducted within applicable legal and regulatory frameworks. Where required, authorized legal professionals may be engaged for formal proceedings.
How long does the recovery process take?+
Timelines vary depending on the debtor’s response, dispute status, and documentation quality. Some cases resolve within weeks, while others may require extended follow-up or formal escalation.
Can CDEF manage multiple recovery cases at once?+
Yes. Businesses can submit multiple outstanding accounts simultaneously. Each case is tracked individually with structured updates and documented communication.
How do I initiate a recovery request?+
You can initiate a request by contacting our team and submitting invoice and transaction details. Once reviewed, our recovery team will outline the structured action plan for your case.

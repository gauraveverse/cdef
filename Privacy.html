Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and financial information when you use our services.

CDEF Collection ("CDEF", "Company", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of the personal and financial information shared with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, store, and safeguard information when you visit www.cdefdebtcollection.com, use our services, or communicate with us. By accessing our website or engaging our services, you agree to the terms outlined below.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide professional debt collection and financial recovery services. This includes:

Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Contact number
  • Email address
  • Residential or business address
  • Identification details (where legally required)

Financial Information

  • Debt and outstanding account details
  • Creditor and debtor information
  • Payment history and settlement records
  • Supporting financial documentation

Website & Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited & time of access
  • Cookies and usage analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information strictly for legitimate business and legal purposes, including:

  • Delivering debt recovery and collection services
  • Verifying identity and preventing fraud
  • Communicating case updates and service notifications
  • Responding to inquiries and support requests
  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations
Important: We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

Sharing of Information

We may share information only when necessary with:

  • Legal advisors and compliance professionals
  • Financial institutions involved in recovery processes
  • Technology and hosting service providers
  • Regulatory or government authorities when legally required

All third parties are required to maintain confidentiality and protect data in accordance with applicable laws.

Data Retention

We retain personal and financial information:

  • For the duration of service engagement
  • As required by law or regulatory authorities
  • For legitimate legal and compliance purposes

After this period, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including restricted access controls and secure server infrastructure, to protect data against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Disclaimer: No online transmission or digital storage system is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance functionality, analyze traffic, and improve user experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though certain features may not function properly.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable laws, you may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion where legally permissible
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications

Requests may be made using the contact details below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. Any such data identified will be removed promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

We recommend checking this page periodically to stay informed of any updates.
